Each Work_Contract represents one part of the future work required to fulfill a Product Ticket as it passes through a supply chain held under the terms of the [For Product](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1cRYLXIQnNWfXNU88UvxdlKgdLfOyoXSvl4xyp6xytMU/) development model.

For example, there would likely be separate Work_Contracts to grow and mill and bake some bread.
